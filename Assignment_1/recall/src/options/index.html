Recall stores everything you read on this device, in this browser, and nowhere else. There is no account, no cloud, no analytics. Search runs entirely in the extension's service worker.

Privacy

  • Your reading history never leaves this browser.
  • Recall has no host permissions — it cannot fetch any URL on its own.
  • Recall has no telemetry. There is no remote endpoint.
  • Your data lives in IndexedDB, scoped to this extension. Uninstalling deletes it.
